Biography

Ji Wang (Senior Member, IEEE) is an Associate Professor and Ph.D. supervisor with the Department of Electronics and Information Engineering, College of Physical Science and Technology, Central China Normal University, China. He received the B.S. degree from the School of Electronic Information and Communications, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2008, and the Ph.D. degree from the School of Information and Communications Engineering, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2013. He held postdoctoral positions with the School of Electronic Information and Communications, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and the Department of Electrical Engineering, Columbia University, USA. His research interests include multiple-antenna communications, space-air-ground integrated networks, integrated sensing and communication, and wireless AI.
